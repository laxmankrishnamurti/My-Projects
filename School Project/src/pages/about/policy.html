Policy & Procedure

1. Admission Application Forms and Prospectus are available at the school campus and can also be Download

2. After submission of the forms, the school will invite parents for a short interaction.

3. Children applying to classes 1-5 will be required to answer a short written diagnostic assessment in English, Mathematics, and Hindi.

4. Submission of the application form does not guarantee admission and will depend on the number of seats.

5. Classes and respective eligibility is as under :

6. Forms duly completed should be deposited with the Proof of residence Photocopy of the Birth Certificate from Municipal Corporation attested by first class officer or First Class magistrate. Two passport-size photographs. The Original documents must be furnished at the time of the meeting. Please note: Transfer Certificate from the previous school will be required after admission has been granted.

7. List of applicants shortlisted for personal interaction and diagnostic assessment will be displayed school.

8. Scheduling of Parent interaction and Student Diagnostic assessment will be sent by email.
