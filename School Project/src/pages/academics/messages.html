Welcome to Lohia Nagar Mount Carmel High School, Patna

Our school vision, Every Student A Respected Member of Society, hinges on our strong belief that every member of this family is a leader, well anchored in the right values, as our school motto encapsulates: Diligence, Prudence, Respectability, and Simplicity. In her quiet ways, a student thrives on challenges and balances them with grace and resilience. As our students embark on their new journey in secondary school, they will discover for themselves a road to self-discovery, development, and fulfillment of potential. Life is a journey on which we want our students to remember where they came from and appreciate what they have gained but we also guide our students to find out where they are going a focus on pursuing their dream. Our students have a remarkable range of talents-both in the academic arena and in the arts, sports, and leadership. In our pursuit of nurturing the whole child, we place great emphasis on our student's holistic learning and growth, encouraging them to be responsive, reflective, and responsible lifelong leaders with a commitment to serve.

As a school with rich tradition and a strong bicultural emphasis, we take great pride in cultivating young leaders, with a deep appreciation and understanding of both Western and Eastern cultures. While deeply rooted in tradition, we celebrate diversity. We set high standards, yet seek to contiguously create and maintain a nurturing and supportive environment in which everyone feels appreciated. We value everyone-our students, staff, Board of Directors, alumni, and parents-who strongly believe in working together. As we soar to new heights, it is these partnerships that secure our place at the very forefront on the global stage.

With best wishes ♥
Late Justice Padma Narayan Singh
